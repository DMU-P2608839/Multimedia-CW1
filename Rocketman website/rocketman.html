SYNOPSIS

Reginald Dwight changes his name to Elton John, collaborates with singer-songwriter Bernie Taupin and becomes one of the most iconic people in pop history. This movie is set around his most beloved songs, showing his breakthrough in the 1970s through a musical story, showing his transformation from a shy piano prodigy to an international superstar
