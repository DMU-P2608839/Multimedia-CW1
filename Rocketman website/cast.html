THE CAST

Taron Egerton

Taron David Egerton is an English actor and singer. He has been nominated for two British Academy Film Awards, a Grammy Award, and a Screen Actors Guild Award. He gained recognition for starring as Gary "Eggsy" Unwin in the action comedy film Kingsman: The Secret Service (2014) and its sequel Kingsman: The Golden Circle (2017). In Rocketman, he plays singer-songwriter Elton John, by doing this, he received critical praise and won the Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Motion Picture Musical or Comedy.

Richard Madden

Richard Madden is a Scottish actor. He was born and raised in Renfrewshire near Glasgow. He rose to prominence in 2011 for his portrayal of Robb Stark in the first three seasons of HBO's fantasy drama Game of Thrones, for which he received the Screen International Award for Star of Tomorrow. Madden made his screen debut as a child actor and later began performing on stage whilst a student at the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland. In 2007, he toured with Shakespeare's Globe company as Romeo in Romeo and Juliet, a role he reprised in the West End in 2016. He plays John Reid in the biopic Rocketman (2019), and a lieutenant in the epic war film 1917 (2019).

Jamie Bell

Andrew James Matfin Bell is an English actor and dancer. He rose to prominence for his debut role in Billy Elliot (2000), for which he won the BAFTA Award for Best Actor in a Leading Role. He plays Bernie Taupin in Rocketman (2019). He earned a BAFTA Award nomination for his performance in Film Stars Don't Die in Liverpool (2017). In television, Bell starred as Abraham Woodhull in the AMC historical drama series Turn: Washington's Spies (2014–2017).
